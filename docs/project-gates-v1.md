# Project Gates v1

These gates are the source for Daily Closeout progress reporting. V1 reports gate state conservatively; unknown is allowed, but the gate source must be explicit.

## 交易系统

Purpose: Help Park use the gold trading system as a safer, clearer personal trading operating system.

Gates:

- System visibility: dashboard/reporting surfaces show current health and useful context.
- Risk safety: live/broker/order-capable paths stay guarded and explicitly approved.
- Review loop: morning/evening trading reviews and alert receipts are fresh and evidence-backed.
- Execution readiness: broker/data-feed readiness is separated from strategy profitability claims.

## Park 的内容生产

Purpose: Turn Park's ideas and AI-tool experience into publishable content assets.

Gates:

- Content asset produced: a draft, script, thread, article, or platform pack exists.
- Packaging complete: the asset has a clear target channel and publishable format.
- Feedback loop: published or reviewed output has evidence, notes, or next action.

## Park OS

Purpose: Keep Park's second brain useful by preserving raw thinking and turning it into structured, retrievable knowledge.

Gates:

- Raw capture: source thinking is preserved without rewriting the original intent.
- Processed layer: useful structure, SOP, diagram, or action layer exists when appropriate.
- Retrieval path: the note lands in a clear Obsidian location with enough context to find it later.

## Agent OS

Purpose: Make Park's agent workflows auditable, repeatable, and safe to automate.

Gates:

- Automation health: scheduled jobs have clear owners, prompts, output paths, and recent receipts.
- Evidence discipline: decisions, gotchas, and outputs are tied to paths, commits, or artifacts.
- Daily closeout: `_daily-closeout.md` and Project `daily-update.md` are refreshed without destroying source artifacts.
- Repo hygiene: code/docs changes are committed only when safe and are otherwise reported as blocked.

## ai newsletter

Purpose: Deliver a useful daily AI/finance intelligence product with visible source health and archive continuity.

Gates:

- Brief generated: the daily brief exists and passes quality validation.
- Delivery verified: Feishu or target channel delivery has a receipt or explicit failure reason.
- Archive updated: Obsidian/archive path contains the final reader-facing artifact.
- Source health visible: stale, disabled, or failing sources remain visible instead of hidden.
